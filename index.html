About

A family pizza shop
on Maple Ave.

We opened in 2019 with one rule: do pizza right, every time. Our dough is mixed in-house and rises overnight. Our sauce uses San Marzano tomatoes — nothing from a can. All meats are 100% halal.

The wings are hand-tossed to order. The subs are toasted. The cannoli is filled when you ask for it. We're a neighborhood place, and we'd rather make ten great pies than a hundred mediocre ones.

— The Pizza Vienna family
